Welcome to BlurTrace

Images shared without consent can cause lasting harm — especially to children and other vulnerable people. BlurTrace lets you blur a sensitive image before you share it, while keeping a secure, traceable link back to the original, so an authorized process can recover the source image later if it's needed.
